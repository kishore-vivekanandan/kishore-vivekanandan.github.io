01 — About

A decade in SAP, building the layer customers actually touch.

I’ve spent more than ten years at SAP, growing from a software developer in Bangalore building test frameworks for SAP CRM, through SAP Health where we shipped a connected healthcare platform, into engineering leadership for the foundations of SAP Business Technology Platform.

Today my teams own BTP Cockpit — the central management UI for every service and environment on BTP — the btp CLI for the dev-ops crowd who live in a terminal, the BTP Agent generative-AI capabilities for operating the platform, and a handful of BTP Neo Core services. The throughline: make the administrative surface of a hyperscale enterprise platform feel humane.

I’m an aspiring organizational architect — I care about building engineering orgs that hold up across very different macro conditions and keep contributing to SAP’s mission of helping the world run better. Outside the day job I mentor intrapreneurs at SAP.iO, write, paint, and have a Guinness record from the world’s largest coding marathon.

02 — Work

What I lead today

The four product surfaces my teams own across BTP Core Experience.

UI /cockpit

BTP Cockpit

The central management UI for every SAP BTP service, subaccount, and environment. The first surface most BTP customers see — and the one my teams keep coherent as the platform grows.

CLI $ btp

btp CLI

A dev-ops & cloud-native, terminal-friendly interface for interacting with BTP services. Built for the people who never want to leave their shell — scripts, CI pipelines, day-two operations.

AI /joule

Joule in BTP Cockpit

Leading the investment case to infuse SAP’s copilot — Joule — into the BTP administrative touch points. Less clicking through trees of services; more describing what you want and getting it.

Agent /agent

BTP Agent — Generative AI for Ops

Generative-AI capabilities for operating the platform itself — translating operator intent into safe, auditable platform actions. Plus stewardship of a few BTP Neo Core services.
